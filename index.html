About

Kesari Betta Math - established by Shree Dwiteeya Samba Shivacharya is more than just a place of worship. It is one of the most prominent religious, cultural and spiritual centers in the region of North Karnataka, India. We emphasize on Bhakti and Dhyana Yoga. People are offered help with their physical, social, emotional and mental issues along with free food everyday.
